
# Nirnai UI Design Blueprint

## 1. Core Principle

Nirnai is NOT a listing site.
Nirnai is a decision engine.

Goal:
Help user decide in <5 seconds.

---

## 2. Page Types

### 1. Homepage (Intent Capture)
- Search bar:
  - "Where are you going?"
  - Dates
  - Guests
- OR paste link
- OR "Ask Nirnai"

---

### 2. Decision Page (Ranking Page)

#### Top Section (Above the fold)

- 🟢 BEST PICK
- Price
- Confidence
- Savings vs next option

Example:

🟢 BEST PICK  
$694 • High confidence  
💰 Save $265 vs next option  

CTA:
👉 Book on Booking.com (Save $265)

---

#### Why This Is #1

- Strong recent reviews
- Best price across platforms
- No safety issues

---

#### Why Not Others

- #2 more expensive
- #3 lower trust
- #4 recent decline

---

### 3. Ranked List (Top 5)

Each item:

- Stamp: 🟢 / 🟡 / 🔴
- Price
- Platform
- Confidence
- 2–3 reasons

Example:

1. 🟢 SMART STAY  
$694 • Airbnb  
High confidence  

2. 🟢 BEST VALUE  
$650 • Booking  
Lower price  

---

### 4. Comparison Layer

- Price difference
- Trust difference
- Recency signal

---

## 3. UI Components

### Decision Stamp
- 🟢 SMART / BEST
- 🟡 CHECK
- 🔴 AVOID

---

### Confidence Indicator
- High / Medium / Low

---

### Savings Highlight
💰 Save $X

---

### CTA Buttons

Replace generic:

❌ Book Now

With:

👉 Book (Save $X)
👉 View Deal
👉 Compare

---

## 4. UX Rules

- No long paragraphs
- Scannable in 3–5 seconds
- Always show WHY
- Always show TRADEOFF
- Always show BEST

---

## 5. Conversion Rules

User must instantly know:

- Is this good?
- Why is it good?
- What should I do?

---

## 6. Mobile First

- Stack layout
- Large CTA
- Highlight savings
- Sticky "Book" button

---

## 7. Future Enhancements

- Cross-platform price comparison
- Same property detection
- Personalized recommendations
- Saved decisions

---

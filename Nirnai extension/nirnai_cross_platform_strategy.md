
# Nirnai Cross-Platform Decision Engine Strategy

## Tagline
Clear decisions. Every purchase.

---

## 1. Core Insight

Users do NOT care about:
- which platform they book from

Users care about:
- getting the BEST option
- balancing price and quality
- avoiding bad decisions

---

## 2. Product Evolution

### Before
Nirnai = Chrome Extension (decision overlay)

### Now
Nirnai = Cross-platform decision engine

---

## 3. Core Value Proposition

> “Find the best option across the internet — not just one platform.”

---

## 4. Decision Framework

### Decision Value =

Trust (confidence)  
+ Price (value)  
+ Quality (experience)  
+ Recency (current reality)

---

## 5. Scoring Model

### Value Score

```
Value Score =
(Trust Score × Recency × Quality) / Price
```

---

## 6. Nirnai Product Flow

### Step 1
Extension detects weak listing

### Step 2
User clicks “Better alternative”

### Step 3
User lands on Nirnai page

---

## 7. Nirnai Page Experience

### Header
Top stays for your trip

---

### Results

- Ranked list (Top 5)
- Each item includes:
  - Platform (Airbnb / Booking / Hotel)
  - Price
  - Trust score
  - Value score
  - 2–3 key reasons

---

### Example

1. SMART STAY  
   High confidence • Stable reviews  

2. BEST VALUE  
   Lower price • Strong trust  

3. CHECK  
   Mixed recent reviews  

---

## 8. Key Differentiator

Airbnb:
- shows listings

Nirnai:
- ranks outcomes

---

## 9. User Experience Principle

Do NOT replace search.

Instead:
- improve decision quality
- reduce effort
- increase trust

---

## 10. Conversion Strategy

- Nirnai curates shortlist
- User clicks “View on platform”
- Nirnai influences decision BEFORE booking

---

## 11. Monetization

- Affiliate links across platforms
- Premium filters (high confidence only)
- Sponsored listings (trust-first approach)
- Future: direct booking integrations

---

## 12. Trust Principles

- Always explain ranking
- Show tradeoffs (price vs quality)
- Avoid bias or hidden sponsorship
- Maintain transparency

---

## 13. UX Guidelines

Each listing should show:

- Stamp (SMART / CHECK / AVOID)
- 2–3 reasons
- Price vs trust tradeoff
- Confidence level

---

## 14. Platform Strategy

Start with:
- Airbnb

Expand to:
- Booking.com
- Expedia
- Hotels

---

## 15. Long-Term Vision

Nirnai becomes:

> Decision OS for consumers

Across:
- shopping
- travel
- food
- services
